# lightShow

just automatic lighting up of LEDs in the order of Red -> Green -> Blue -> All and then all of them turn off.

## Parts used
- 1 Arduino UNO
- 1 Breadboard
- 1 usb cable
- 1 RGB LED
- 5 Red LED
- 5 Green LED
- 4 Blue LED
- 7 220ohm resistors
- 15 M-M cables
